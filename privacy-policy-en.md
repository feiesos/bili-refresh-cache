## How We Collect and Use Your Personal Information

Personal information refers to various information recorded electronically or otherwise that can identify a specific natural person's identity or reflect their activities. We are pleased to inform you that **our Product does not collect any personal information about you**.

The only data stored by the Product is a local cache of video metadata (such as titles, URLs, author names, and cover image links) that you voluntarily encounter while browsing bilibili.com. This data is stored exclusively on your device via `chrome.storage.local` and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

## How We Store and Protect Your Personal Information

Since our Product does not require personal information, we are pleased to inform you that **we do not store any personal information about you**.

All video metadata cached by the extension is stored locally in your browser's built-in extension storage (`chrome.storage.local`). This data remains on your device and can be cleared by you at any time through the extension's interface.
